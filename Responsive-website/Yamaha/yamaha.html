HISTORY

YAMAHA
Yamaha Corporation is a Japanese multinational corporation that specializes in manufacturing a wide range of products, including musical instruments, electronics, motorcycles, and power sports equipment. The company was founded in 1887 by Torakusu Yamaha, who initially repaired a broken reed organ and later went on to manufacture his own reed organs1. In 1900, Yamaha produced its first piano, which was the first piano to be made in Japan2. Since then, the company has expanded its product line to include a wide range of musical instruments, audio equipment, and other products.
